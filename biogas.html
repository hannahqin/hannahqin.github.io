ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Biogas International is part of BLUElab, a U-M student organization composed of twelve teams with one united goal: To engineer technology that addresses issues of environmental sustainability and development. The Biogas International Team specifically focuses on developing biogas technology to help restore the environment, improve health outcomes, and achieve economic sustainability.

The team was looking for a logo and branding that set them apart from other indivdual BLUElab teams, while remaining cohesive with the overarching identity of BLUElab.
