WNY Women-Owned Business Directory

January-March 2021

ABOUT THE PROJECT

As a volunteer consultant for WNY Women's Foundation, I was recruited to design and build a custom experience where people can easily browse a directory of over 200 women-owned businesses in Western New York. I worked with the organization to quickly deliver a solution that was aligned with their brand vision, built within their existing website infrastructure, and that could be easily maintained without my ongoing intervention.

ABOUT WNY Women's Foundation

WNY Women's Foundation is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to mission is to create a culture of possibility so each woman can live, grow, and lead to her fullest potential. They focus their efforts in Western New York, where they are based.
