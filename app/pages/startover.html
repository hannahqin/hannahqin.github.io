"If I Could Start Over" – Mentality Magazine

Fall 2016

ABOUT THE DESIGN

I designed this spread for Mentality Magazine, a student publication at the University of Michigan dedicated to discussing mental health. By providing a platform where students could openly share their experiences with mental health, we hoped to educate the campus community about mental health and combat the stigmas associated with it.
