I'm a creative who works across physical and digital mediums, from handcrafted jewelry to web experiences to aerial acrobatics.

BIO

Currently, I run a small business making and selling embroidered creations. I also co-founded Track Trans Legislation, a tool to help track and organize opposition towards anti-trans legislation proposed across the United States. Previously, I worked as a Product Manger at Microsoft and an English teacher in France. In my free time, you can find me rediscovering my love for oil painting and developing new aerial silks choreography.
