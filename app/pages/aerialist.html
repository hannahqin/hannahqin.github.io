ABOUT AERIALIST

AeriaList is a skills-tracking app for people who train aerial arts (aka aerialists). In the aerial world, some skills are more intricate than others, but all skills require an acute understanding of the exact steps involved in order to perform it safely. One of the challenges faced by aerialists of all levels is that it can be difficult to remember all of the steps required for all of the skills theyâ€™ve learned. With AeriaList, aerialists can easily record these steps alongside a reference photo or video in a personal glossary of skills.

THE PROJECT

AeriaList was designed as my final project in the Digital Product Design course at the University of Michigan.
