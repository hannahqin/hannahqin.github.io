ABOUT TRACKIT

Trackit is an easily digestible way for CS-LSA majors at the University of Michigan to view their progress towards graduation and plan out their class schedules accordingly. With an overview of your unfulfilled requirements, a detailed display delineating completed and in-progress requirements, and an interactive schedule planner, staying on track for graduation has never been easier.

THE PROJECT

I developed trackit in a team of four as our final project in EECS 493 (User Interfaces). We were given a month and a half to develop a web-based UI product from ideation to implementation. The process included storyboarding, creating a working prototype, conducting user research, iterating, and demonstrating our final product.

ROLE

Project manager, UI/UX designer, and the primary developer of the schedule planner

LANGUAGES

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, AngularJS
