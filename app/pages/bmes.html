ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

BMES is a student organization comprised of biomedical engineering students interested in networking with industry, peers, faculty, and alumni. They offer a variety of industry and research panels, social opportunities, and hospital service opportunities. They hope to bring greater university recognition to the Biomedical Engineering Department while creating a sense of community among the department's students.
