THE PROJECT

As a volunteer at Start Small Think Big, I advised small, low-income businesses on web solutions to help grow their market access. KG's Bagel Chips was one of my clients; I worked with them to design a custom e-commerce site tailored to their business needs and aligned with their brand vision.

ABOUT KG'S BAGEL CHIPS

KG's Bagel Chips is a Brooklyn-owned small business selling delicious chips made from New York bagels. By using bagels that can no longer be sold past their one-day shelf life, KG's Bagel Chips brings you that famous New York bagel taste while making a positive impact on the NYC food system and environment.
