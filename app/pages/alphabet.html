"The Alphabet of High Fashion" Poster

October 2016

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This poster was designed as a part of the Graphic Design (SI 320) course at the University of Michigan. We were presented with the challenge of designing a themed English Alphabet Poster using consistent visuals, colors, and typography. Each grid element had to be composed of a capital letter, a label, and an image, and only one typeface could be used for the design.
