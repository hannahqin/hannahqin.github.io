ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Track Trans Legislation was built in response to the alarming rise in anti-trans legislation being proposed in state legislatures across the country. These bills threaten to prevent trans kids from participating in sports, deny gender-affirming healthcare, ban gender identity education in schools, and more.

With Track Trans Legislation, users can access an interactive map and table to easily understand the status of anti-trans bills across the country, learn how to take action to defend trans rights, and generate sample scripts to use for contacting legislators.
